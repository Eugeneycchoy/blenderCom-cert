Certification

The Blender Certification Exam aims to provide a clear competency assessment standard, allowing candidates to demonstrate their expertise in Blender. Many claim to "know Blender", but often lack an objective evaluation standard. This certification establishes distinct proficiency levels, serving as a reference for individuals and the industry.

Certificate Structure

The Blender Certification Exam follows a structured framework based on the Blender Badge system, which divides skills into six core categories, each with progressive proficiency levels. The certification structure ensures a clear and measurable evaluation of skills

  • * Level 0 and Level 1 exams are available for candidates.
  • * Level 2 and Level 3 exams will be introduced in future phases of the program.
