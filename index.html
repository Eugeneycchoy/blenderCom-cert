Certification

The Blender Certification Exam aims to provide a clear competency assessment standard, allowing candidates to demonstrate their expertise in Blender. Many claim to "know Blender", but often lack an objective evaluation standard. This certification establishes distinct proficiency levels, serving as a reference for individuals and the industry.

Certificate Structure

The Blender Certification Exam follows a structured framework based on the Blender Badge system, which divides skills into six core categories, each with progressive proficiency levels. The certification structure ensures a clear and measurable evaluation of skills

  • * Level 0 and Level 1 exams are available for candidates.
  • * Level 2 and Level 3 exams will be introduced in future phases of the program.

Certification Procedure

01

Exam Registration

Candidates begin by logging into their Blender Community account. After logging in, they can choose their desired skill category and register for the online exam for the corresponding certification level.

02

Exam Preparation

Candidates can access preparation materials to ensure readiness for the exam. These resources include Blender manual and our training courses on www.blenderstudio.cn

03

Taking the Exam

To begin the exam, candidates must log into their account and click on the "Take Exam" button to activate the online exam.

Read More

04

Scoring and Results

Each exam consists of 20 multiple-choice questions, with each correct answer worth 1 point. To pass, candidates must achieve a score of 75% or higher (at least 15 correct answers out of 20).

Read More

05

Certification

Passing candidates are awarded a digital certificate that serves as an official recognition of their proficiency in the selected skill category and level.
